    Component Selection

    3.3V voltage regulator

    Image Product Price Pros Cons
    LM1117T-3.3/NOPB $1.61 high current overhead (800mA) Integrated heatsink bulky
    LP2950-33LPE3 $1.37 compact Pins are closer together for peralta milling, may require spreading
    LE33CZ-AP $1.01 Compact Pins are well spread cheap none

    Decision: The LE33CZ-AP will be chosen for it’s size, cost, and ease of assembly with the peralta mill specs. Since the LCD subsystem and level shifters won’t draw more than 10mA, the 100mA rating is more than sufficient.

    5V voltage regulator

    Image Product Price Pros Cons
    LM7805 N/A Sufficient current (1.5a) Integrated heatsink Already procured bulky
    AS78L05RTR-E1 $0.13 Compact Integrated heatsink Cheap Insufficient current capacity
    NJM7805FA $1.01 Sufficient current capacity Easy soldering No heatsink

    Decision: The LE33CZ-AP will be chosen for it’s size, cost, and ease of assembly with the peralta mill specs. Since the LCD subsystem and level shifters won’t draw more than 10mA, the 100mA rating is more than sufficient.

    Speaker

    Image Product Price Pros Cons
    SP-1511S-3 $2.37 Compact Moderate volume Inexpensive Requires tight soldering Up-firing, complicates enclosure design No waterproofing rating
    CSS-40408N $12.31 Very loud Possibly waterproof (not rated Easy soldering Expensive Not very compact on board
    SPKM.10.8.A $2.31 Low price Very loud Low power draw compact Not waterproof

    Decision: The SPKM.10.8.A will be chosen due to it’s low size, high volume, and inexpensiveness being ideal for the application of an alarm system.

    LCD Screen

    Image Product Price Pros Cons
    NHD-0216K1Z-NSW-BBW-L $7.92 Programming is clearly described 4-bit more to save lane count 2 lines of characters Built in controller May not show well in bright light or an angle
    NHD-C0220BIZ-FSW-FBW-3V3M $11.41 Easy to read in bright light 2 lines of characters Built in controller More complicated but well documented programming No 8-bit mode for higher control
    NHD-0420AZ-FSW-GBW-33V3 $13.64 4 lines of characters Easy readability in bright light Built in controller Optional 4 bit mode Requires more space on enclosure

    Decision: The NHD-0420AZ-FSW-GBW-33V3 will be chosen to allow for more complex data or text to be displayed. The cost is slightly higher than other models, but the increased readability, size, and well documented programming offsets this.

    Switch button

    Image Product Price Pros Cons
    3-1825910-5 $0.16 Inexpensive Moderate height for clearing enclosure Thin surface, no lettering options
    84SN-CC3-000 $22.87 Convenient packaging Allows for more complex input Large button surface Expensive Large
    B3F-4050 $0.26 Inexpensive Allows for a keycap to be mounted Requires a keycap to be designed with proper tolerances

    Decision: The B3F-4050 will be chosen to allow for efficient enclosure design, cost, and ease of use by providing a labeled and large button surface via custom keycap.

    Switch Debouncing

    Solution Pros Cons
    Custom debouncing circuit Guaranteed functionality regardless of PIC state Simple to design Requires board space Adds complexity to BOM and circuitry
    Software debouncing Saves board space Reduces hardware complexity Requires a timer circuit. Since there is no OS on the PIC, only one hardware timer is available. Since scheduling method is unknown, all program must be run as a single thread, or the debouncing may not be accurate May not function at startup of PIC

    Decision: A custom debouncing circuit will be chosen due to the complexity of porting an OS (such as FreeRTOS) to the PIC to share timer resources and the option to have the user use buttons on the PIC’s startup to enter debugging modes.

    Level Shifters

    Image Product Price Pros Cons
    V62/24621-01XE $3.48 Provides 6 circuits,simplifying board Simple pin layout Fast switching Will not arrive in time, out of stock SMD
    CD40109BPWR $0.46 Does not require high voltage supply Only has 4 circuits, so 2 units will be required SMD
    74AXP2T45DCH $0.77 Compact THT Unidirectional Only has 4 circuits, so 2 units will be required

    Decision: The 74AXP2T45DCH will be selected due to it’s ease of soldering and compactness. While bidirectional capability would help with future modification, it is not worth the tradeoffs for this project.
